Pitch Black is a (currently incomplete) expansion mod for Rain World that's been in development for a little over a year by me—Lurzard—and my friends on the Pitch Black Dev Team. It's part of its unique AU (Alternate Universe) That manipulates pre-established lore for Rain World and its DLC to tell a new story.

The current [BETA] of Pitch Black was made public way earlier in development and is available for people who want to look at the experience now and see what to expect in the finished version.

Though it's just something for us to build off of, feedback is appreciated! It has two new Slugcats with unique abilities and mechanics, a custom nighttime-looking world with new creatures for it, new threat themes for every explorable region, a cute new overseer to guide you, some tiny lore changes, and more.
