Pitch Black is a WIP expansion mod for Rain World that's been in development for a little over a year by the Pitch Black Dev Team. It is part of an AU that manipulates pre-established lore for Rain World and its DLC to tell a new story.

The current [BETA] of Pitch Black was made public way earlier in development and is available for people who want to look at the experience now and see what to expect in the finished version.

Though it's just something for us to build off of, feedback is appreciated! It has two new Slugcats with unique abilities and mechanics, a custom "night" world with new creatures for it, new threat themes for every explorable region, a cute new overseer to guide you, some tiny lore changes, and more.



The [BETA] of Pitch Black has 2 new campaigns to play, while unable to be completed, they are fully implemented with finished abilities and mechanics. They are...


The Photomaniac:
A maniacal juvenile without a mother. Who's only begun to develop tiny whiskers. Although small, they have some powerful tactics involving their electrical nature. With the use of their electric saliva, advanced weaponry of spears can be made at the cost of food.

The Beacon:
A matured slugcat with long whiskers. Frail but swift, they can move quickly from region to region. They can create flashbangs out of rocks at the cost of food. In this darkened world, they make a great defense against the creepy crawlies, and against their relentless pursuer.
